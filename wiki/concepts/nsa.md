# NSA — New Sale Announcement

The internal notification that a new retirement plan has been sold and is entering the conversion pipeline. The NSA is the trigger that starts the [[com]] workflow.

## Definition

When a plan is sold and assigned to begin conversion, a New Sale Announcement (NSA) is issued internally. The COM receives this notification and uses it as the starting gun for all subsequent conversion activity — scheduling kickoffs, creating the project plan, and opening the case.

## Why It Matters

Nothing formally begins until the NSA is received. It marks the transition from "sale" to "implementation" and is the COM's first signal that a plan is real and active. The DC, by contrast, is notified through [[nbi]] and a manager assignment — the NSA is COM's equivalent entry point.
