# RDD Form

A payroll-related form that originates with [[tc]], is routed through the client by the [[com]], and submitted back to TC upon completion.

## Definition

The RDD Form is a TC-authored document that must be completed by the client as part of payroll setup. The COM acts as the routing intermediary — receiving the form from TC, sending it to the client, following up for completion, and submitting the completed form back to TC.

"RDD" acronym meaning is not yet confirmed.

## Workflow

1. TC creates and provides RDD Form to COM
2. COM sends RDD Form to client for completion
3. Client completes form and returns it to COM
4. COM submits completed form to TC

## Why It Matters

The RDD Form is part of the payroll setup process and depends on a functioning COM ↔ TC ↔ client loop. If the client is slow to return it, payroll setup can be delayed. COM must track this handoff actively.
