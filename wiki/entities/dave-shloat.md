# Dave Shloat

The team's data systems expert — Alteryx and Informatica authority on the Large Market team.

## Overview

Dave Shloat is the data guru on the Large Market team. His domain is the data toolchain: [[alteryx]] and [[informatica]] are his territory. If you have a question about how data flows, how a workflow is built, or how to troubleshoot a data processing issue, Dave is the person to ask. He is a deep and accessible resource — a massive pool of knowledge that is genuinely useful to the team.

## Key Facts

- **Team:** Large Market
- **Domain expertise:** [[alteryx]] (data blending/transformation) and [[informatica]] (ETL workflows, including Day of Wire and Participant Balance)
- **Contact type:** Direct — go to him with data tool questions

## Role in Data Operations

Dave is the go-to for anything touching the data infrastructure. For DCs, this is most relevant when working with [[informatica]] workflows (Conv File, Day of Wire, Participant Balance) or any Alteryx-based data processes. He understands how the tools work at a technical level and can diagnose problems that are otherwise opaque.

He is a core piece of the team's operational capability — the kind of resource whose knowledge is hard to replace and easy to tap.
