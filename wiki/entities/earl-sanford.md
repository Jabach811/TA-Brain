# Earl Sanford

Lead Data Consultant and primary training resource for new DC hires. 25+ year TA veteran.

## Overview

Earl Sanford is the Lead Data Consultant on the Large Market team. He is the person in charge of training new employees and serves as the main day-to-day support resource for incoming DC cohorts. He is the first stop for questions — on process, systems, edge cases, or anything else that comes up in the day-to-day work of a DC. He has been at TA for over 25 years, which means his institutional knowledge runs deep.

Earl leads the training of new Data Consultants. For a new hire, he is the primary human anchor: the person to shadow, to ask first, and to check with when something doesn't look right. His long tenure means he has seen nearly every edge case and exception. He is also a strong team player — colleagues across the team rely on him for guidance, not just new hires.
