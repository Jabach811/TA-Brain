# Ellen Miller

Internal contact who manages the plan assignment process on the NBI side, reaching out to DC managers when new plan slots need to be filled.

## Overview

Ellen Miller acts as the liaison between the NBI system and DC managers when new plan assignments come in with empty spots. She notifies managers of available assignments so they can distribute plans to appropriate Data Consultants.

## Key Facts

- Contacts DC managers (not DCs directly) when new plan assignments have unfilled DC slots
- Part of the [[nbi]] assignment pipeline
- Role appears to be on the operations/coordination side rather than data

## Role in the Assignment Process

When a new sale assignment comes out and gets loaded into NBI with empty DC spots:
1. Ellen Miller reaches out to the DC's manager
2. Manager reviews caseload, conversion type, and capacity
3. Manager assigns the plan to a specific DC
4. Plan is now officially on the DC's radar

## Additional Notes

- **Department:** Unknown — she communicates across many departments
- **Team:** Likely has a team (not confirmed)
- **Plan types:** Handles **all plans** — not limited to specific types or market segments
