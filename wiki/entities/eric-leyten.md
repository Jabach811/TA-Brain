# Eric Leytem

Key contact on the [[ftp-team]] who handles the final step of FTP account setup and sends confirmation to the DC.

## Overview

Eric Leytem is the DC's primary point of contact on the FTP Team. Once the FTP Team completes account setup for a payroll vendor, Eric sends the confirmation that the connection is live and ready. He is also the go-to contact for FTP status updates and any setup questions.
