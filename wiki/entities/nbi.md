# NBI

Internal system where new plan assignments are loaded and tracked. The starting point for every DC's involvement in a new plan.

## Overview

When a new sale is finalized, the plan assignment is entered into NBI. NBI tracks which DCs, COMs, and other roles are assigned to each plan. Empty spots in NBI trigger [[ellen-miller]] to reach out to managers for assignment.

## Key Functions

- Tracks plan assignments by role (DC, COM, etc.)
- Flags unassigned ("empty") slots
- Acts as the official record of who owns what

## Assignment Flow

1. New sale → loaded into NBI
2. Empty DC slots detected → [[ellen-miller]] notifies DC manager
3. Manager reviews caseload and distributes to a DC
4. Plan is now tagged to the DC in NBI

## DC Interaction

NBI is a **universal checklist** for every plan. Every time anything happens — a document received, a change made, a milestone hit — it gets logged in NBI.

Structure:
- **Sections per role:** DCs, TCs, and other team members each have their own checklist sections within the plan's NBI record
- **Notes field:** Space for free-form notes on each entry
- **Date stamps:** When things were done
- **Universal visibility:** Anyone can jump into NBI to check the current status of any plan

The DC updates NBI continuously throughout the conversion — not just at the end. Every milestone, receipt, and action is logged.

## Key Facts

- **Full name:** New Business Intelligence
- **Access:** Web-based tool
- **Visibility:** DCs can see **all plans** — not just their own. History is also visible.
- **Checklist structure:** All standard items — not free-form. Every DC works from the same checklist structure.
