# Rich Lippoth

Go-to contact for incoming share activity and regular fund trades — particularly time-sensitive Vanguard trade approvals.

## Overview

Rich Lippoth is the primary contact when trades need to be approved or coordinated beyond normal processing windows. His involvement is most commonly triggered during participant balance posting when Vanguard funds are involved and the FMC deadline has already passed.

## Key Facts

- **Primary function:** Approves fund trades — especially incoming shares and regular trade activity
- **Vanguard deadline:** If Vanguard funds are on the Fund Management Calendar and the 2:00 PM EST update deadline is missed, Rich must approve via email (followed up by IM or phone) before trades can proceed
- **Scope:** Not limited to Vanguard — go-to for incoming shares and regular trade situations broadly

## Role in Conversions

Rich is engaged during the participant balance posting phase when:
1. Vanguard funds are involved and the FMC wasn't updated by 2:00 PM EST
2. Any trade-related issue requires approval beyond the standard cutoff window

Standard flow: Email Rich → follow up by IM or phone.
