# AAA

**Full Name:** Adoption Agreement Addendum

## Definition

The AAA is a plan document addendum used in the PEP (Pooled Employer Plan) structure. It appears in the COM's TRS Plan Documents section for PEP plans — likely an addendum to the adoption agreement executed when an employer joins a PEP.

## Used By

- [[roles/com]] — COM handles AAA document routing in the PEP plan documents workflow
- PEP plans only
