# AWD

**Full Name:** Automatic Work Distribution

## Definition

AWD is Transamerica's internal work-ticketing and routing system. Teams submit AWD tickets to request actions from other departments — data corrections, payroll handoffs, rebooking requests, and more. Tickets are routed automatically based on ticket type and department.

## Used By

- [[lm-dc]] — submits tickets to [[prod-support]] for data corrections, to initiate payroll handoffs (FILESPECRQ type), and for rebook actions
- [[com]] — submits and receives tickets throughout the conversion lifecycle
- [[tc]] — receives and flips AWD settings (e.g., contribution monitoring)
- Most internal teams touch AWD at some point in a conversion
