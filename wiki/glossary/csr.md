# CSR

**Full Name:** Conversion Summary Report

**Definition:** A post-conversion report produced by the Quality Consultant at go-live. The [[com]] receives it and forwards it to the client as a formal summary of the completed conversion.

**Used By:** Quality Consultant (produces it); COM (routes it to client).
