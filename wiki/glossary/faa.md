# FAA

**Full Name:** Fund Asset Allocation

## Definition

FAA refers to the fund asset allocation data requirements that the DC must satisfy during a conversion — the specific data fields and formats the prior record keeper must provide for fund-level allocation processing.

## Used By

- [[roles/lm-dc]] — DC is responsible for gathering and processing FAA data from the prior RK
- [[entities/eds]] — FAA data is loaded through EDS layouts
- [[entities/informatica]] — allocation module processes FAA data
