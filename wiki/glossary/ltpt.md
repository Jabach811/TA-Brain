# LTPT

**Full Name:** Long-Term Part-Time (Eligibility)

## Definition

LTPT refers to the Long-Term Part-Time eligibility category established by the SECURE Act 2.0. Effective January 1, 2025, employees who work at least 500 hours in two consecutive 12-month eligibility computation periods (minimum age 21) become eligible to make elective deferrals to a 401(k) or 403(b) plan — regardless of the plan's standard eligibility requirements.

LTPT employees may only make **elective deferrals**; they are not eligible for employer contributions unless they also meet standard plan eligibility.
