# NSA

**Full Name:** New Sale Announcement

**Definition:** The internal notification issued when a new retirement plan has been sold and is entering the implementation pipeline. Receipt of the NSA is the trigger that starts the [[com]] workflow.

**Used By:** COM (primary recipient); likely originates from a sales or business development team.
