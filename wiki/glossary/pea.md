# PEA

**Full Name:** Plan Enrollment Agreement

## Definition

The PEA is the enrollment agreement document used in the PEP (Pooled Employer Plan) onboarding workflow. It appears in the COM's OBP (Onboarding Package) process for PEP plans specifically.

## Used By

- [[roles/com]] — COM routes the PEA as part of the PEP onboarding package workflow
- PEP plans only — does not appear in standard non-PEP conversions
