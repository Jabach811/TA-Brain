# TM

**Full Name:** Transition Manager *(deprecated — now COM)*

## Definition

TM (Transition Manager) was the former name for the role now called [[com]] (Communications Coordinator / Conversion Operations Manager). Older training materials, emails, and documentation may reference "TM" or "the TM" when referring to the person who schedules meetings, manages the PRD/subpack, routes approvals, and coordinates external communications during a plan conversion.

All current references use **COM**. TM is retained here only to aid interpretation of legacy documents.

## Used By

- Historical: DC training materials pre-dating the rename
- Current equivalent: [[com]]
