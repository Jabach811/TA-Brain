# VEF

**Full Name:** Vendor Expense Form

**Definition:** A document used when a vendor expense arrangement needs to be established for a plan. Routed by the [[com]] through an advisor firm and the plan sponsor, then submitted for internal L&C system setup.

**Used By:** COM (routes the workflow); advisor firm (completes the form); client/plan sponsor (signs it); L&C team (sets up the arrangement in the system).
