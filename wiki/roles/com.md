# Communications Coordinator (COM)

The project manager for every retirement plan conversion at Transamerica. The COM owns the full lifecycle from NSA receipt through go-live — running meetings, managing documents, coordinating all internal teams, and serving as the primary client-facing contact.

## What This Role Does

The COM is not just a meeting scheduler — they are the **document factory, routing hub, and go-live coordinator** for each plan conversion. Every task that crosses team lines runs through COM.

**Phase by phase:**

### 1. Kickoff
- Receives the [[nsa]] (New Sale Announcement), which triggers the entire COM workflow
- Schedules and runs the internal kickoff call with the full project team
- Schedules and runs the external kickoff call with the client and all parties
- Creates the [[project-plan-com]] (format depends on plan type: Active Project Plan for non-PEP, Excelwise for PEP)
- Uploads project plan to [[nbi]]; sends copy to conversion team
- Sets up weekly internal and external update calls for the duration of the conversion
- Sends recap emails after every call (internal and external)

### 2. Prior Recordkeeper Coordination
- Requests prior plan documents from Plan Services; routes them through [[ellen-miller]] to the docs team for review
- Tracks the document review pipeline through to completion
- Creates and sends the [[prior-carrier-letter]] to the plan sponsor and notifies the prior carrier directly
- Creates Successor Trustee Letter if requested

### 3. Letters and Notices
- Creates all participant-facing communications: [[blackout-notice]], cover letter, mapping notice
- Receives QDIA Initial Notice and Fee Disclosure from [[tc]]
- Creates Privacy Notice
- Manages the plan sponsor review and approval loop for all notices
- Creates the notice mailing file and routes the notice request through [[ellen-miller]]
- Coordinates Conversion Newsletter review with [[doc-ops]] (communication consultant function); collects address and email files
- For non-PEP: creates Transition Communications Checklist and routes to Samantha C.

### 4. Vendor Expense Form (if applicable)
- Sends [[vef]] to advisor firm → collects approval → sends to client for signature
- Submits L&C ticket via SF for setup; collects confirmation

### 5. Pricing Review
- Completes pricing review during the internal call
- Emails pricing review template to team

### 6. Payroll
- Confirms whether payroll functionality (WX V2) is needed
- Sends payroll specs; runs payroll call with all parties
- Coordinates test file receipt and pass/fail with DC
- Notifies [[jen-curtin]] for 360° payroll plans
- Sends Payroll Questionnaire to client; forwards completed questionnaire to [[lm-dc]]
- Manages [[contribution-monitoring-form]] (TFS plans): sends to client, receives back, routes to TC for AWD flip, saves to FILESERVE
- Manages [[rdd-form]]: receives from TC → sends to client → receives back → submits to TC

### 7. Loan Analysis (if applicable)
- Requests loan information during the client kickoff call
- Routes loan analysis request to the Quality Consultant (QC)

### 8. Early Access (if applicable)
- Requests early access file template from [[lm-dc]]
- Routes template to client for completion; receives and submits back to DC
- Verifies with DC that early access file has been loaded
- Notifies [[tc]] to activate early access the day before it starts
- Sends email to plan when early access is open

### 9. On-Boarding Package — PEP Plans Only
- Receives OBP from docs team; adds signed [[toa]]; updates package tabs
- Fills out PEA (plan enrollment document — see Open Questions) and Info Sharing Form
- Sends OBP, PEA, and Info Sharing Form via [[docusign]] for client signature
- Receives signed package; saves copies to Documents folder, CIT, and DCGLIB
- Routes copy to TRSC IM Plan Signed Documents

### 10. TRS Plan Documents — PEP Plans Only
- Coordinates internal plan doc review, then client review (PRD call)
- Receives AAA, Secure 2.0 amendment, and SPD from docs team
- Sends all three to client via [[docusign]] for signature
- Saves executed documents; emails to TRSC IM Signed Plan Docs

### 11. Sub Pack — Non-PEP Plans
- Creates [[subpack]] from standard template
- Distributes to the full internal project team
- TC reviews during the internal call; TC maintains the subpack after review

### 12. Enrollment Guide
- Requests enrollment guide from [[tc]]
- Sends enrollment guide to client

### 13. WX / Plan Sponsor Site
- Confirms whether plan is set up on [[wx]]
- If not, emails TC to set up WX access

### 14. Auto Enrollment Review (if applicable)
- Reviews auto enrollment during the internal call
- Sends auto enrollment recap email to relevant parties

### 15. Trust Agreement (if applicable)
- Completes Trust Agreement Request
- Submits to Plan Services Workflow Team

### 16. Assets Received
- Receives confirmation from [[lm-dc]] that assets were received and deposited
- Sends email to plan confirming asset receipt and amount

### 17. Go-Live
- Participates in internal go-live review (TC typically sets up)
- Sends go-live email to client
- Completes Auto Delivery Form (non-PEP)
- Sends post-go-live email to internal team for final steps
- Adds plan to Survey Spreadsheet
- Completes Audit Checklist in [[nbi]]
- Sends SPD to [[ellen-miller]] for participants (PEP only)
- Receives [[csr]] from Quality Consultant; forwards to client
- Adds NBI Transition out of CIT Date
- Notifies [[ellen-miller]] that the plan is LIVE

### 18. Final Call Reminders
- Reminds client that a survey will be sent
- Introduces Account Manager to client
- Reviews any open items and payroll reminders

## Key Handoffs

**Comes in:**
- NSA from sales pipeline → triggers COM workflow
- Prior plan documents from Plan Services → COM routes to Ellen → docs team
- QDIA notice, fee disclosure from TC
- RDD form from TC
- OBP from docs team (PEP)
- AAA, Secure 2.0, SPD from docs team (PEP)
- Enrollment guide from TC
- Early access file template from DC
- Asset receipt confirmation from DC
- CSR from Quality Consultant at go-live

**Goes out:**
- Project plan → NBI + conversion team
- Prior carrier letter → plan sponsor + prior carrier
- Participant notices → plan sponsor (approval) → Ellen Miller (mailing)
- Payroll questionnaire → client → DC
- Contribution Monitoring Form → client → TC
- RDD form → client → TC
- Early access file → DC
- OBP, PEA, Info Sharing Form → client via DocuSign (PEP)
- Plan documents → client via DocuSign → TRSC IM (PEP)
- Subpack → internal team → TC for maintenance (non-PEP)
- Go-live email → client
- CSR → client
- SPD → Ellen for participants (PEP)

## What Other Teams Should Know

- **COM is the hub.** If something needs to get to the client, it goes through COM. If something needs to get from the client to an internal team, it goes through COM.
- **COM owns timing.** The project plan and meeting cadence are COM's. If the conversion is running behind, COM is the first to know and the one to escalate.
- **COM creates the subpack (non-PEP) but doesn't maintain it.** TC takes over after the internal call review. A missing or incomplete subpack means COM created it but never shared it — or never created it at all.
- **COM's go-live checklist is thorough.** By go-live, COM has touched every team and every document. The NBI audit checklist at go-live is COM's final sign-off.
- **Two plan-type tracks run in parallel.** PEP plans have OBP/PEA/plan documents workflows that non-PEP plans do not. Non-PEP plans have subpack and Auto Delivery Form workflows that PEP plans do not.
