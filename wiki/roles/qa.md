# Quality Assurance (QA)

Audits imported data and validates mappings throughout a conversion; approves fund/source mappings and clears eligibility.

## What This Role Does

QA provides a verification layer on data imported during conversions. They audit data that DCs bring in from prior record keepers, review and approve source and fund mappings, receive pre-eligibility notifications, and do post-processing checks after final files are posted. Their core focus is **data audit and mapping validation** — catching errors in imported data before they persist into production.
