# Transition Consultant (TC)

Handles higher-level, systematic back-end work during a plan conversion; reviews mappings and approves eligibility alongside QA.

## What This Role Does

The TC operates more on the back-end/systematic side of a conversion, as distinct from the COM who is more front-end/client-facing. TC reviews fund and source mappings, is CC'd on key communications, and has their own checklist section in NBI.

> [!note] This description is from the DC's perspective only. A full TC brain dump is needed to document this role accurately.

## Who They Work With

| Team | Nature of Relationship |
|------|----------------------|
| [[lm-dc]] | CC'd on mapping approvals and eligibility notifications; NBI checklist partner |
| [[qa]] | Often CC'd together; parallel approval track |
| [[com]] | Works within COM's communication structure |

## Key Handoffs

**Comes in:** Fund mapping, source mapping (via COM), eligibility notification

**Goes out:** Mapping approvals, eligibility go-ahead (alongside QA)
